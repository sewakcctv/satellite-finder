How to calibrate compass offset 1. Find a clear view of the southern sky (from Canada/USA).
2. Look up the azimuth of a GEO satellite for your location (e.g. Bell TV / Anik F1R at 107.3°W).
3. Point your phone at it and adjust the offset slider until the label aligns.
